---

## 🌱 Core Principles

### 1. Ownership

All Real-Work Data remains the intellectual and moral property  
of its human creator(s).

No individual, organization, or automated system may claim ownership,  
reproduce, or resell this data without explicit written consent  
from MaMeeFarm or the original contributor.

---

### 2. Usage Rights

Public materials may be used for:

- non-commercial research
- educational purposes
- documentary reference

Commercial use, resale, redistribution, or AI model training  
**requires explicit written authorization** from MaMeeFarm.

All permitted uses must preserve attribution  
and contextual integrity of the original data.

---

### 3. Attribution

All derivative or referenced works must include the following acknowledgment:

> Source: MaMeeFarm — Proof-of-Human-Work Ecosystem (MMFARM-POL-2025)

Failure to provide clear attribution constitutes a violation of this license.

---

### 4. Ethical Boundaries

The following practices are prohibited:

- Manipulative data reuse
- Undisclosed synthetic data mixing
- Exploitative algorithmic deployment

AI training, dataset construction, or data transformation  
must respect the dignity, consent, and context  
of the human contributor.

MaMeeFarm reserves the right to revoke data access  
upon breach of ethical or contractual obligations.

## ⚙ Protocol Context

MMFARM-POL-2025 operates within the broader  
**DGCP (Data Governance & Continuous Proof) framework.**

Under DGCP:

- Real-Work Data is recorded through verifiable evidence structures
- Data provenance is maintained through append-only records
- Licensing records remain traceable and auditable

## 🔒 Legal Status

Real-Work Data may constitute a form of  
**Human Intellectual-Labor Asset (HILA)**.

Such assets may be protected under applicable copyright law,  
moral rights doctrine, and international data governance frameworks.

Unauthorized use may constitute infringement  
of human-authored digital property.
